About Me

Starting from my humble beginnings in a small town, called Goshen, Ohio, I had no experience with game design or programming and I was at a loss for what I should persue in college. I had heard of Shawnee State Universitiy's Game Design programs and I thought about how I was already acceptional at mathemetics and how I had a passion for video games and I decided to give it a shot. Little did I know I would fall in love with my major and quickly rose to the top of my class. Playing a great video game was good, but coding my own character, designing my own levels, etc. was that much more satisfying to me. Even just coding a non-game related piece of software was fun, and I was surprisingly exceptional at it.

I now have an endless passion and drive to code quality software. I am willing and I desire to push myself to learn new things and broden my horizons as far as they can go to become not just a better programmer, but a better designer. I have an eye for detail and I know what feels good in a game, and that has been my winning combination.
